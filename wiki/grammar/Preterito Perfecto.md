# Pretérito Perfecto (Recap)

## Rule
Used for actions that happened in the past but are connected to the present or occurred in a time period that includes the present (e.g., *este mes*, *últimamente*).

## Usage Examples (Recaps)
- **Recent Action:** "...desde el club azulgrana ya **han dado** los primeros pasos."
- **Recent State/Change:** "El rendimiento del delantero **ha generado** dudas en los últimos meses."
